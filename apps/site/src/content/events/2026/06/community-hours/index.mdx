Join us for this Community Half Hour focused on the Tool Editor in BDC Powered by Seven Bridges. Have a working script locally or within a Data Studio session? Does it take multiple hours to run, do you want to run it across multiple files, or do you want to scale? Learn how to convert your script into a CWL app using the Tool Editor. We’ll talk through key features, efficient tool testing, and even touch on creating a workflow (multiple connected tools).

Even if you cannot attend the session live, you can still [register](https://renci.zoom.us/webinar/register/WN_Ep1GKhMDTwav4gKCPmp7CQ). Materials and videos will be distributed in the June BDC newsletter. Sign up to receive the newsletter [here](/join-bdc/).
