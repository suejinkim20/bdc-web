Join us for this short, half hour webinar session where we will feature three short presentations. Register even if you cannot attend, to ensure you receive the recordings.

- **From the BDC Coordinating Center**: apply for (free) pilot cloud credits. Pilot cloud credits are available to start your analysis on BDC.These credits are enough to complete some entire analysis on BDC for some researchers. If you haven't applied for credits yet, this brief demonstration will show you how. 
- **From BDC Powered by PIC-SURE**:  see the new Advanced Query Builder in action. We'll walk through how to combine AND/OR logic, use subqueries, and refine complex queries to retrieve the cohort to support your research. 
- **From BDC Powered by Terra**: A recent improvement optimized how data tables are implemented for increased performance and scalability. We will be showcasing how researchers can export data from BDC Powered by Gen3 and use data tables and workspace data to facilitate inputs and outputs for workflows, demonstrating how customized workflows are easy to write and deploy.

Even if you cannot attend the session live, you can still [register](https://renci.zoom.us/webinar/register/WN_B7oQdiImRt2w3fUSHjEU0w). Materials and videos will be distributed in the June BDC newsletter. Sign up to receive the newsletter [here](/join-bdc/).
