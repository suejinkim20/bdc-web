Mark Effingham, Ph.D., M.Sc., is the Deputy CEO of UK Biobank and brings broad experience in technical strategy, IT delivery, and domain expertise in health informatics and big data. He is a Chartered IT Professional and a Fellow of the British Computer Society. He holds a Ph.D. and M.Sc. from the University of Birmingham.
