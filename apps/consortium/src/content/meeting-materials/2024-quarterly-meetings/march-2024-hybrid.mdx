## Overview

The BioData Catalyst Coordinating Center was proud to host the March '24 BioData Catalyst Face-to-Face meeting in Bethesda, MD on March 11 & 12, 2024. **111** people attended the event (49 in person), consisting of BDC consortium members, NHLBI staff, Imaging research presenters, and other partners.

**Day 1**focused on providing the consortium with updates to the Workplan 4.0 structure, including work done to date at NHLBI to perform a M&O cost deep dive, and updates to existing Working Groups. The day also saw conversations focused on updates of the NHLBI ECO group and associated consortium team member activities, and presentations by Gen3 and Seven Bridges staff demonstrating recent improvements to their respective platforms. The remainder of the day focused on data ingest discussions, with a special eye towards understanding and beginning to address the possible Imaging data challenges coming to BDC.

**Day 2**provided updates to the consortium on behalf of recent workshops and group meetings including the EEP February meeting on LLM applications and the TOPMed All Hands Debrief. Additionally, there was a robust conversation around migration the TOPMed Exchange Area to BDC. We also received updates from all teams on their New Development work done to date, and heard from EEP members and NHLBI staff on their thoughts coming out of the meeting.
