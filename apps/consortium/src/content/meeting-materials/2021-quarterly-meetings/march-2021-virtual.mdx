***ZOOM***was proud to host the tenth BioData Catalyst “Face-to-Face” meeting and the fourth conducted entirely virtual. Over 100(!) BioData Catalyst consortium, Fellows, and NHLBI members attended across the two-day meeting focused on User Education & Engagement, Fellows Cohort I & II progress, Fellows Cohort III introductions, program partnerships and interoperability (RAS, GA4GH, NCPI, PASC), developing a diverse scientific community, and pending changes/challenges across all BioData Catalyst initiatives.
