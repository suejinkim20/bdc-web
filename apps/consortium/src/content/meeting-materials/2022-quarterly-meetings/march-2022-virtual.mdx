_ZOOM_ was proud to host the fourteenth BioData Catalyst “Face-to-Face” meeting and the eighth conducted entirely virtual. We took a look back at significant achievements and contributions made during WP3.1, while also looking ahead to planning for WP3.1.1, WP4.0 and BDCatalyst v2. Please find linked below **notes** and **slides** shared across the two-day event, along with links to the **meeting recording**.

Our save the date for the next meeting is June 1 - 2, 2022 -- in Chapel Hill, NC (fingers crossed!)
