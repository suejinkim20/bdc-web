The DataSTAGE Coordinating Center was proud to host the second DataSTAGE Face-to-Face meeting in Chapel Hill, NC on March 19 & 20, 2019. 65 STAGE consortium and NHLBI members participated in-person with an additional 8 virtual attendees.

Both meeting days focused on Working Group and Tiger Team Breakout sessions. These productive meetings enabled consortium attendees to reach a basic level of consensus and deeper understanding. Each day the WG's/TT reported back to the whole group with a status of the work accomplished that day and a summary of their next steps. A schedule to finalize the DataSTAGE [Strategic Plan](https://drive.google.com/open?id=1Pp8PQJNSfh8uPvKKt0PN7d1dsLD-ofTtv-HUSBxdsTQ), [Implementation Plan](https://drive.google.com/open?id=1NyPFOSUMgF4mhISa3F8yHlyoaKq49qp2HDdoeMalX24), and [Project Management Plan](https://drive.google.com/open?id=1yvENgYxGBzluJY-hJeVrpDocGVkQEjnGzZXpCfel6Fs) was also discussed on Day 1.

Day 2 was kicked off with an insightful presentation by keynote speaker [Professor Reichman](https://law.duke.edu/fac/reichman/), from the Duke University Law School, on [Sharing by design: Data and decentralized commons](http://science.sciencemag.org/content/350/6266/1312). Jerome's presentation was followed by a discussion around preparing for the June 4th presentation to NHLBI Council, more WG/TT breakout sessions, and concluded with very helpful thoughts from the DataSTAGE External Experts Panelists: [Dr. David Mendelson](https://nhlbidatastage.org/contact-card/show/146), [Jason Williams](https://nhlbidatastage.org/contact-card/show/256), & [Dr. Warren Kibbe](https://nhlbidatastage.org/contact-card/show/254).

Prior to the 2-day consortium meeting, the Data Harmonization Working Group met for a half-day workshop on Monday, March 18th, also in Chapel Hill, NC.
