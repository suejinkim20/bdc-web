STAGE-RFC-4: Workplan 3.0 DataSTAGE User Narratives describes agreement among participants in the STAGE Consortium on the User Narratives that will be addressed by March 2022. The purpose of the RFC is to formalize and circulate this agreement to all stakeholders DataSTAGE program.

The STAGE-RFC-4: Workplan 3.0 DataSTAGE User Narratives shall provide information for all STAGE Consortium members and stakeholders as to the agreed User Narratives and associated Epics and Features for September 2020 and User Narratives for March 2022. Subsequent RFCs will be released to capture agreement on later collections of User Narratives. Additional context, background, and notes are available in the [STAGE User Narratives - Decomposed document](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1jdz6nSvlQCgep9Pp_ajp9AWS5t6FB1PGidJmVZjPrpE/edit?usp=sharing).
