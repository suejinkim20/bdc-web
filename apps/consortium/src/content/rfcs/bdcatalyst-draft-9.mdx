[Closed] This DRAFT RFC is for changes that break the integration between the ecosystems of BioData Catalyst, namely Terra, Gen3, Dockstore, Seven Bridges, PIC-SURE and the HeLx applications. Each ecosystem is responsible for managing this problem for changes that impact modules internal to their systems.
