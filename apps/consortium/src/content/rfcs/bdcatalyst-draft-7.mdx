This Data Generator Guidance document is one component of a series of data management documents for Principal Investigators and study data owners. These documents aim to describe how data within the BioData Catalyst Ecosystem is managed, ingested, and made available, including metadata generation, data preservation, data security, and ethics, in alignment with NIH policies and the FAIR (Findable, Accessible, Interoperable, Reusable) data principles.
